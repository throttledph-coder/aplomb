PRODUCT VISION
What the App Is
Aplomb is a resume-aware, real-time interview assistant that sits on your desktop during a job interview. You upload your resume and paste the job description before the interview. The app uses that context to generate answers as if you personally wrote them — matching your experience, your words, your background.

Free users type questions manually and get instant personalized answers. Premium users get automatic listening that catches what the interviewer says and generates answers hands-free, with smart filtering that ignores filler words and non-questions.

Who It Is For
Active job seekers preparing for or currently in interviews
Career changers who need help positioning transferable skills
Non-native English speakers who want polished, natural-sounding answers
Nervous interviewees who freeze under pressure
Anyone interviewing at companies above their current level
